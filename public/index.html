Who I am

Development + Design

Since beginning my journey as a graphic designer almost 10 years ago, I've collaborated with talented people and have done remote work for many startups. Today I continue to hone my skill by designing and developing digital products.

I was introduced to web development while creating wireframes and designing the graphics and user interfaces of various websites and applications. I sought to extend my skills by learning how to not just design layouts, but build them as well. I learned Full-Stack Web Development while attending Lambda School of Software Engineering and Computer Science.
