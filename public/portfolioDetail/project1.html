Cliquebook
Invite members to sign your organization's handbook
Description :
Cliquebook is a group project built with React.js that involved coming up with a solution for organization leaders that would allow them to invite their members through email, using a magic link, and be notified when the member has viewed and signed the handbook. The handbook can be customized in many ways including fonts, drag and drop section reordering, and image uploading. Technologies used include React Quill, Stripe, Cloudinary and Multer to name a few, click this link and try it out!
