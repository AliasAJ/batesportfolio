Cliquebook
Invite members to sign your organization handbook
Description :
Cliquebook was a group project that involved coming up with a solution for organization leaders that would allow them to invite their members through email using a magic link and be notified when the member has viewed and signed the handbook. The handbook can be customized in many ways like fonts, section reordering and images. Technologies used include React Quill, Stripe, Cloudinary and Multer to name a few, click this link to take a look for yourself!
