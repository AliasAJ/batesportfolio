Reciprocity Coaching
Your personal guide through life's many challenges
Description :
Jeremiah Payne approached the Grounded Web team and I to build an identity for his life coaching services. The project included a logo, business card and stationary as well as a website. The logo incorporates a calming blue infinity loop to signify that the kindness you pay forward is returned to you. This message is carried throughout the branding for all of Jeremiahs print materials and website design. If you're in need of someone that can help give you that push through life's obstacles, visit reciprocitycoaching.com.
